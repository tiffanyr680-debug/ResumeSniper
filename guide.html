Quick Start Guide

Step 1: Set Your Key Qualifications

Tell the Sniper engine what words you are looking for (e.g., "Master License," "Journeyman," "EPA Certified"). The engine will weigh these heavily in the scoring.

Step 2: Bulk Upload Resumes

Export your candidate list from Indeed or LinkedIn as a CSV or Text file. Drag it into the Sniper engine. Our local logic will scan 100 candidates in under 30 seconds.

Step 3: Call Your Golden Matches

Candidates with a score over 90% are highlighted in emerald. Click "Call Now" to view their details and book an interview before your competitors do.

# 🎯 ResumeSniper PRO

**Automated Candidate Scoring & High-Speed Recruitment Intelligence.**

ResumeSniper PRO is a local-first enterprise tool designed for business owners, HR managers, and trade specialists. It eliminates the friction of hiring by instantly ranking candidates based on certifications, experience, and custom keywords.

## 🚀 How to Use the System

Follow these three steps to find your next "Golden Match" hire:

1. **Set Your Target Benchmarks:** Open the engine and define your requirements (e.g., "Master License," "10+ Years Experience," "OSHA Certified"). Our logic engine will weigh these as your top priorities.
2. **Bulk-Process Applications:** Drag and drop your candidate list (CSV or Text format) into the uploader. The system scans the entire batch locally in your browser memory.
3. **Contact the Top Tier:** Candidates with a match score of **90% or higher** are automatically highlighted in Emerald. Click "Call Now" to immediately view their phone/email and book your interview.

- **2026 GDPR Ready:** By processing data locally, you remain compliant with the strictest global privacy laws.
